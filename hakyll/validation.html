I'm looking forward to meeting you at the Data Validation workshop in Edinburgh.

I had promised to provide a Docker image but decided against it, sorry for any inconvenience that may cause.

Here are the necessary steps to prepare your computer for the workshop:

  • Download Node.js (at least version 14):
    https://nodejs.org/en/download

  • Clone or download the Git repo with the workshop code:
    https://github.com/NicoleRauch/ValidationCode

  • Invoke `npm install` in the repo's top level directory

  • Optional: Install an IDE, e.g. WebStorm:
    https://www.jetbrains.com/webstorm/
    (free 30-day trial available)

And that's it!
